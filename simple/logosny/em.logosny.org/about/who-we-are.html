Who We Are

Logos Community Church (LCC or Logos, pronounced LAH-gahss, for short) is a thriving non-denominational church called to worship God in spirit and truth, to nurture the spiritual growth of believers, and to spread the mercies of God and gospel of Jesus Christ to the community.

We are one church with two congregations organized by language (English and Chinese). This site is dedicated to the announcements, news, and events of the English Ministry. Our English Ministry community is made up of individuals ranging from youth to families with children.
