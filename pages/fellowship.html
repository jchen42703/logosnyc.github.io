FELLOWSHIP

YOUTH

Our youth fellowship consists of middle and high school students who attend various schools in Manhattan, Bronx, Queens, and Long Island. Friday Fellowship is one of the best connection points for students with the church and an opportunity for them to encounter Christ alongside their peers in an open, fun, and challenging environment. A typical evening includes worship, games, teaching, and small groups.


The youth fellowship meets on Fridays at 7:30 PM in the Chapel.


For more information, contact Keith, keith@logosny.org.

COLLEGE

Our college fellowship consists of students who attend schools across New York as well as out of state students who return during their breaks. The college fellowship seeks to be a place where students can discover what it means to follow Christ and how to align their vocation as a student with God as they encounter new freedoms, decisions, and responsibilities.


The college fellowship meets on Fridays at 7:30 PM in Room 204.


For more information, contact Mary, mary@logosny.org, join the Facebook group or view the College Fellowship website.

CAREER

Our career fellowship consists of young adults and adults (20’s to 30’s) who are navigating a particularly transitory stage of their life. Some transition from one career to another. Others transition from singleness to marriage. In career fellowship, brothers and sisters find a community that seeks to challenge them to walk in a manner worthy of the gospel and the calling to which they have received as followers of Jesus Christ.


The career fellowship meets on Fridays at 7:30 PM in Room 203.


For more information, contact Keith, keith@logosny.org.

FAMILY

Our family fellowship consists of parents with children ranging from infants to teens. The fellowship seeks to help families form their identity in God through understanding God’s purposes for families, parenting, and marriage. We meet to learn what the Bible says about these issues and to read Christian books on marriage and parenting.


The family fellowship meets every third Saturday at 6:30 PM in Room C03.


For more information and to know where the next meeting location will be, contact Keith, keith@logosny.org.
