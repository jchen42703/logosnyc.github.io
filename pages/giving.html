HOW TO GIVE

Tithly

Donate easily just by clicking on our church's tithly link. After clicking the link, pay either with credit card or debit card, or bank account.

Give through Tithly

Check

Please mail it directly to our church: 43-00 171st Street Flushing, NY 11358 . The check should be made out to Logos Community Church. Feel free to drop a note in to specify a cause you want the offering to go to (missions, general fund, stewardship).

In-Person

Come for in-person service and we accept offering at the beginning of service after worship team is finished playing.
