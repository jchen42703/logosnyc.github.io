OUR MISSION

The mission of LCC is to build God-centered disciples who live out God’s Word and to lead people to Christ.
The vision of LCC is to be a church that nurtures God’s children through God’s Word and brings God’s love
and salvation to individuals and communities, both locally and globally.

OUR STAFF

Our English Ministry is led by our two amazing pastors. If you’re looking for someone to blame, you’ve found the right spot.

KEITH LOO

ENGLISH MINISTRY PASTOR

Keith has served on staff as the English Ministry Pastor at Logos Community Church since its inception a few years ago. Prior to entering full-time ministry, Keith worked as a structural engineer for many years and then attended Alliance Theological Seminary for his Master of Divinity. However, rather than hanging up AutoCAD for a Bible, God has continued to use Keith’s skills and experiences as a structural engineer in his ministry through the construction and renovation of Logos’ new building.

MARY LEUNG

Lay Pastor

Sensing God’s call to ministry, Mary attended Alliance Theological Seminary for her Master of Divinity. She currently serves with the college fellowship at Logos. While not working directly with college students, Mary continues to serve God through her passion for worship leading and missions, having served in places like Romania.
